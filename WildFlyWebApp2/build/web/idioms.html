Norwegian idioms and adverbial phrases.

    Below are some examples of idioms and adverbial phrases with their English equivalents:

Some idioms:

    foelge noen/noe med argusoeyne - watch sb/sth closely

    himle med oeynene - roll one's eyes

    med sneglefart - at a snails pace

Some adverbial phrases:

    alt i alt - on the whole

    i forgaars - the day before yesterday

    i timevis - for hours
